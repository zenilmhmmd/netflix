Popular Movies

Deadpool and Wolverine

comedic, action-packed movie about a former special forces operative turned mercenary who gains healing powers and sets out on a quest with wolverine

Shawshank Redemption

A powerful drama about hope and friendship, following Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly imprisoned, who forms a bond with fellow inmate Red while finding ways to endure and defy the harsh realities of prison life.

The Dark Knight

A gripping superhero thriller where Batman faces his greatest challenge as the Joker unleashes chaos in Gotham City, testing the limits of justice, morality, and the hero's own resolve.

LA LA land

"La La Land" is a vibrant musical romance that follows the love story between an aspiring actress and a jazz musician in Los Angeles, as they pursue their dreams and face the challenges of success and sacrifice.

Home Alone

is a classic family comedy about a young boy named Kevin who is accidentally left behind when his family goes on vacation. He must protect his home from two bumbling burglars using clever traps and tricks.

jerry maguire

is a romantic comedy-drama about a sports agent who experiences a moral epiphany, leading him to start his own agency with a single client and a loyal assistant. As he navigates the ups and downs of his career and personal life, he learns about loyalty, love, and the value of genuine human connections.

Titanic

is an epic romance and disaster film set against the ill-fated maiden voyage of the RMS Titanic. The movie follows the love story of Jack and Rose, two passengers from different social classes, as they navigate their romance amidst the looming tragedy of the ship's sinking.
